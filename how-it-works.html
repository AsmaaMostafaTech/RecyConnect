How RecyConnect Works

From Surplus to Value in 3 Easy Steps

1

Collect Surplus

Restaurants, households, and farmers submit their surplus items through our platform. Simply list what you have available, and we'll take care of the rest.

2

Redistribute & Upcycle

Items are either redistributed to charities in need or upcycled by skilled housewives into valuable new products, creating economic opportunities.

3

Track & Impact

Track your environmental impact in real-time. See how much waste you've diverted from landfills and the positive change you're making in your community.
